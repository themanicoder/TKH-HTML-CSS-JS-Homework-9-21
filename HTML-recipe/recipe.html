Simple Fried Rice

Fried rice is a quick and delicious way to transform leftovers into something greater than the sum of its parts. Though we sometimes think of certain ingredients being typical (eggs, garlic) the only things you need to make fried rice are heat, rice, and oil. Anything else you add is up to you. Before you start cooking a batch though, read our tips below first!

Ingredients

Serves 2 people

  • INGREDIENTS
  • 3 tbsp. sesame oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 green onions, seeded
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp. peeled and minced ginger
  • 4 cup cooked left over rice
  • 3/4 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tbsp. low-sodium soy sauce

Preparation

  • Heat a large cast iron skillet over high heat until very hot, about 2 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon oil.
  • Beat egg with 2 teaspoons water and a large pinch salt and add to skillet. Transfer to a plate when the eggs are cooked
  • Return the pan to a high heat and add 2 tablespoons oil, and the veg. Cook until lightly golden, about 2 minutes. Add garlic and ginger , cook until fragrant and do not burn
  • Add rice, peas, and cooked eggs to skillet. Pour in soy sauce and cook, stirring until heated through, 1 minute. Season with salt and pepper and stir in the green onions. Maybe add chicken as a protein option
